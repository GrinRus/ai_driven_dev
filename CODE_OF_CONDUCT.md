# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and constructive in feedback.
- Assuming good intent and asking clarifying questions before escalating.
- Focusing on technical outcomes, not personal attacks.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, discrimination, or insulting/derogatory comments.
- Trolling, intimidation, or deliberate disruption of discussions.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct and may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this policy.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately via repository maintainers. Security-related reports must follow `SECURITY.md`.

## Enforcement Guidelines

Maintainers may apply any action they deem appropriate, including warning, temporary ban, or permanent ban from project spaces.
